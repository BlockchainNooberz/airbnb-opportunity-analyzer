# Airbnb Opportunity Analyzer 🏠🌍

> Data-driven Python analyzer for short-term rental markets, Airbnb investment strategies, and community development plays

Maps high-yield Airbnb markets globally, models ROI by city, identifies regulatory-friendly zones, and surfaces community development opportunities tied to the short-term rental economy.

## What It Does
- Ranks global cities by Airbnb revenue potential (ADR × occupancy)
- Models cash-on-cash returns for different property types and markets
- Flags regulatory risk by jurisdiction
- Identifies community development and co-living opportunities in Airbnb-adjacent markets

## Strategy Categories
- **Urban Arbitrage** — lease-to-sublease in high-demand metros
- **Co-Living Conversions** — convert multifamily to hybrid short/mid-term rental
- **Rural Glamping** — capitalize on outdoor travel boom
- **Community Development** — Airbnb-anchored neighborhood revitalization
